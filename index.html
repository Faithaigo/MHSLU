Lightening Your Load Because YOU MATTER!

VISION

To improve the lives of those living with mental illness by providing adequate emotional support

MISSION

To develop, implement and maintain a comprehensive unified system to enhance mental health awareness, promote individual empowerment, and increase access to treatment and other services

OBJECTIVES

  • To increase awareness and understanding of mental health
  • To encourage young people suffering quietly to speak up and seek help
  • To provide access to affordable and sustainable mental health services.
  • To be the community voice influencing government decision making in regard to mental health

About Us

Founded in 2021, Mental Health Support League Uganda is an organization that recognizes mental health as an essential part of overall wellbeing. We envision a climate for Mental Wellbeing that is a model for the rest of the nation.

We understand that mental illness, just like any other physical illness, is a treatable condition. It can develop in anyone. It is preventable and those who experience mental illness can return to good health.

We acknowledge that what Mental Health needs today is more sunlight, more candor, and more unashamed conversations. We need so much more openness, transparency and courage to talk, as well as the understanding that "It is okay not to be okay."
